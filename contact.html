Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about contacting the office

How quickly can I expect a response?

Most inquiries receive an initial response within 48 hours during business days. For urgent media requests, please mark your message as "Media Deadline" in the urgency field.

Do you accept speaking engagement requests?

Yes, Comrade Ajaero accepts select speaking engagements. Please use the speaking engagement option in the inquiry type and include details about your event, date, and audience.

How do I request an interview?

Media interview requests should be sent through the form with "Media Interview" selected. Please include your outlet, proposed topics, and deadline.

Can I submit feedback or ideas?

Absolutely! We welcome feedback and ideas from Nigerians. Select "Feedback" in the inquiry type and share your thoughts.
